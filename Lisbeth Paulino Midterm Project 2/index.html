Lisbeth's Cafe

We serve brunch & dinner!

Discover a hidden gem in the heart of the coast, where nature meets flavor at our one-of-a-kind cafe nestled within the confines of a rocky haven, overlooking the vast expanse of the ocean. At our establishment, we specialize in the rich and diverse flavors of authentic Dominican cuisine. Our menu caters to all cravings, from a delightful brunch to a memorable dinner. Enjoy a delectable variety of seafood and meat dishes that showcase the true essence of Dominican culinary artistry. If you have a sweet tooth, or simply yearn for the flavors of the Dominican Republic, our assortment of pastries and traditional delicacies will transport you to the streets of Santo Domingo. Join us for an extraordinary dining experience where the sea's soothing melodies blend harmoniously with the vibrant flavors of the Dominican Republic.
